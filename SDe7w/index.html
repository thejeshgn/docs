Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy should be read in conjunction with the Terms of Use available at [ Terms of Use ]. This Privacy Policy will govern the use and access of the mobile portal entitled ‘MobileOne’ [ https://www.mobile.karnataka.gov.in ] (“Portal”) by You.
